Privacy policy

How OpenMontage Space handles planner, checkout, and analytics data.

Details

OpenMontage Space collects operational events such as page views, CTA clicks, billing toggles, checkout starts, paid-gate hits, and planner submissions when D1 is configured. The public preview should not receive secrets, raw private footage, payment card data, or confidential production material.

Checkout is handled by Polar hosted checkout. Payment details are entered with the provider, not stored in this site. Support requests can use support@aigeamy.com.
